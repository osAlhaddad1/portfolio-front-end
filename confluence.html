Context

Thierry Bachez, the managing partner of la confluence in namur, faced a common challenge: finding qualified and motivated staff. to tackle this, he decided to digitalize part of the restaurant’s menu.

The goal was to create a user-friendly solution for managers, waiters, and customers. it needed to be accessible simultaneously from multiple locations while maintaining a simple, functional, and elegant design.

Solution

We developed a qr code menu system connected to the restaurant’s printers, streamlining the ordering process. each menu item is stored in a database and can be sent directly to the kitchen, saving valuable time.

Result

This innovative solution saves an average of 3 minutes per order on the terrace. with 12 tables using the system, this translates to over 40 daily tickets. waiters now make fewer unnecessary trips and can focus on providing better service to customers.

STRATEGY TO WORK ON THIS PROJECT

The team crafted a design that exuded elegance and simplicity, ensuring the concept was conveyed with clarity.

Menus were thoughtfully arranged, and soothing colors were selected to create a visually relaxing and user-friendly experience.

The website was meticulously programmed from the ground up and, upon launch, received enthusiastic approval from users.

Brasserie de la Confluence

The Brasserie de la Confluence is a 2.0 version of the traditional brasserie.

At our place, the menu is composed exclusively of local Belgian products.

The culinary proposal is intended to be local, in direct contact with the producers while respecting the seasonality of the products.

This collaboration, based on trust and continuous improvement, allows us today to offer our customers a cuisine called "from seed to plate".

We guarantee total transparency on the origin, quality, sustainability of all ingredients from an environmental, social and economic point of view.

Conviviality is at the heart of the gastronomic experience, do not hesitate to ask us to share the different dishes offered.

Enhancing Our Impact: The Three Pillars of Sustainability

Our journey towards sustainable food is anchored by three vital pillars: social, environmental, and economic. We aim to deliver nutritious food that cherishes biodiversity, promotes fairness, and remains economically accessible to all.
What is Sustainable Food?

Imagine a food system that guarantees food security and nutrition for everyone, without jeopardizing the essential economic, social, and environmental foundations for future generations.

This means being cost-effective, economically sustainable, socially beneficial, and environmentally friendly.

Key Pillars:

  • Ecological: Nurturing nature by ensuring a positive or neutral impact on natural resources.
  • Social: Creating widespread benefits that uplift society.
  • Economic: Maintaining cost-effectiveness and economic sustainability throughout the process.
