The relationship between FANATYXH and followers

FANATYXH is an innovative platform designed to strengthen the bond between sports teams and their fans.

It offers real-time match analysis, exclusive content, and personalized experiences. By integrating social media and augmented reality, it aims to amplify fan excitement on match days and maintain engagement year-round

Fans can share game screenshots, vote on match outcomes, stay updated on events, and discuss challenges, creating a vibrant community.

STRATEGY

The team envisioned a unique logo, drawing inspiration from the circular gear of Tyche, the Greek goddess of luck, and incorporating elements that suggest speed. After exploring numerous designs and iterations, we finally crafted a logo that perfectly encapsulates their vision.

Development & website

We began by crafting a bespoke wireframe, meticulously outlining the sections and overall layout of the site.

With a foundation in user experience principles, we infused the design with artistic flair, selecting vibrant colors that echo the project’s dynamic, sports-centric theme.

The typography was chosen to complement this energetic vibe, and we incorporated elements that elevate and enhance the core concept.
