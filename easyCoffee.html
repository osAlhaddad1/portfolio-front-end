Easy Coffee

A company that specializes in renting coffee machines via e-commerce.

Home AboutUs Services Blogs Projects ContactUs

Context

Easy Coffee was seeking a creative digital agency to develop its visual identity and manage the programming and operation of its e-commerce website.

Solution

An integrated system was developed for the company that meets all its needs, using Odoo as the core system.

It supports many of the required features such as customer management, orders, and invoices.

Account management and inventory alerts.

Result

We were able to enhance customer loyalty through a smart discount system for repeat purchases.

The system is flexibly designed to scale their business, giving them the ability to seamlessly add new products, machines and customers.”

With real-time updates and low-inventory alerts, the rental coffee machines ensure that they run efficiently, minimizing unnecessary downtime and ensuring a great customer experience.

These technology solutions have enabled the company to run its business efficiently and seamlessly.

STRATEGY

We've streamlined processes using Odoo, which provides:

  • A user-friendly website Designed to showcase the company's products according to UI/UX rules.
  • Customer accounts: Seamless account creation for users, facilitating future purchases.
  • Discount system: A smart discount feature that rewards loyal customers with automatic discounts based on their purchase history.
  • Order management: A streamlined checkout process with order tracking available on the user's account dashboard
  • Invoicing: Automatically generate invoices for every purchase, with easy access to invoices through the user account or via email.

Esay Coffee

Efficient Coffee Machine Rental Management System

Comprehensive coffee machine rental management:

Manage rental data: A customized dashboard to handle machine rentals, tracking tenant information, machine numbers, rental duration, and locations.

Inventory Tracking: Inventory levels of coffee, milk and sugar are monitored in each rented machine.

Shortage alerts: Automatic notifications are triggered when inventory falls below a set limit, ensuring timely refills.

Data synchronization: Regular updates of machine data to reflect real-time inventory and utilization statistics.

Special orders and billing: A separate process to handle special orders and invoices with ease.
